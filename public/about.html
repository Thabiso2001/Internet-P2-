Who We Are

The Department of Information & Communication Technology at Mangosuthu University of Technology is a premier institution dedicated to excellence in ICT education. We prepare students for successful careers in the rapidly evolving technology landscape.

Our programmes are designed in collaboration with industry leaders to ensure our graduates are workplace-ready. We focus on both theoretical knowledge and practical skills, giving our students a competitive edge in the job market.

  • ✅ Industry-recognised qualifications
  • ✅ State-of-the-art computer laboratories
  • ✅ Work Integrated Learning (WIL) opportunities
  • ✅ Strong industry partnerships
